# 20 Months of Love 💕

A heartfelt webpage celebrating 20 months together, created after returning from 2 magical months in the Philippines.

## About This Project

This project was made with love to express how much I miss my girlfriend after spending 2 beautiful months together in the Philippines. It captures the bittersweet feeling of being back home in Chile while my heart remains with her.

## Features

- ✨ **Beautiful intro screen** with a heartfelt message
- ✈️ **Animated airplane** flying across the screen, symbolizing the journey between us
- ⭐ **Twinkling stars** background creating a romantic atmosphere
- 💕 **Floating hearts** throughout the page
- 📏 **Distance visualization** showing the 17,730 km between Chile and Philippines
- 📊 **Time together stats** celebrating our 2 months together
- 💌 **Love letter** expressing all my feelings
- 💍 **Promises section** with commitments for our future

## Themes

- **Missing you**: The pain of being apart after being together
- **Beautiful memories**: Celebrating the 2 months we shared
- **Hope for the future**: Looking forward to her coming to Chile
- **Eternal love**: Our love transcends any distance

## How to View

Simply open `index.html` in any modern web browser.
